Project Manager

At D’Accubin, our Project Managers are the driving force behind the successful execution of our projects, ensuring that we deliver exceptional results that help grow our clientele. As a Project Manager, you will play a critical role in coordinating and leading cross-functional teams to deliver projects on time, within scope, and within budget. Your expertise in strategic planning, meticulous attention to detail, and exceptional problem-solving skills will be key in transforming complex ideas into seamless executions that elevate our clients' digital presence.


You will act as the primary point of contact for our clients, building and maintaining strong relationships to ensure that their needs are met and their expectations exceeded. Your ability to communicate effectively with stakeholders and manage project risks will be essential in delivering high-quality outcomes that drive client satisfaction and loyalty.


By fostering a collaborative environment and inspiring your team, you will ensure that every project is executed to the highest standard, setting D'Accubin apart as a leader in the industry. Your leadership and dedication will be instrumental in growing our clientele and contributing to our long-term success.

Responsibilities

  • Develop comprehensive project plans that outline scope, timeline, resources, and budget. Coordinate with internal teams and clients to ensure clear communication and alignment on project objectives.
  • Lead, mentor, and inspire cross-functional teams, fostering a collaborative environment that encourages innovation and accountability. Ensure team members understand their roles and responsibilities and have the support they need to succeed.
  • Serve as the primary point of contact for clients and stakeholders, providing regular updates on project status, addressing concerns, and managing expectations. Build strong relationships to facilitate smooth project execution and client satisfaction.
  • Identify potential risks and develop mitigation strategies to minimize impact on project timelines and deliverables. Proactively address issues and roadblocks, ensuring that projects stay on track.
  • Ensure that all deliverables meet the highest quality standards and align with client requirements. Conduct thorough reviews and quality checks at each project phase.
  • Stay up-to-date with industry trends and best practices. Identify opportunities for process improvements and implement changes to enhance project efficiency and outcomes.

Qualifications

  • Demonstrate solid experience in creating and managing sophisticated digital projects at a leading digital agency, either in the capacity of Project Manager or a similar position.
  • Significant experience in collaborating with cross-functional teams and stakeholders, with the ability to provide and take constructive feedback to drive positive results.
  • Ability to manage and inspire teams from diverse backgrounds and responsibilities, ensuring effective collaboration and project success.
  • Expertise in developing strategic project plans and executing them to produce impactful outcomes across various digital, marketing, and social media platforms.
  • Knowledgeable about how strategic project management solutions work and how they can be tweaked to meet client requirements satisfactorily.
  • Experience in overseeing project deliverables with great attention to detail, ensuring accuracy and consistency across all phases of the project.
  • Consistent ability to work in a fast-paced environment, handle multiple tasks and projects, and deliver exceptional quality work under pressure and strict deadlines.
  • Possess the ability to help clients understand how project plans will come to fruition while adopting a flexible approach to harmonize ideas with our clients’ vision for their brands.
  • Be a confident communicator who can articulate ideas clearly, both internally and externally, using relevant support.
  • In-depth knowledge of project management software (e.g., Asana, Jira, Trello) and methodologies. Familiarity with design and digital tools, including Adobe CS and MS Office Suite, is a plus

Benefit

  • competitive salary and performance-based bonuses.
  • opportunities for continuous learning and development.
  • Flexible work options, including remote work.
  • A supportive and dynamic team environment.
